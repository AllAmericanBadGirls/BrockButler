How can I find my classes?

To set directions in the map
Click on maps and then click on the menu button and select search for location, and enter the block or room number and pick your destination then click on the menu and select get directions

To search for location
Click on maps and then click on the menu button and select the search for location menu item and then select the search location to be added to the map

To update position
Click on the maps and then click on the menu button and then select the update position
